The Challenge Before Us

Systemic Issues with Current AI Models

  • Factual Unreliability: Large language models frequently generate inaccurate information, drawing from unvetted sources across the internet
  • Information Currency: AI responses often rely on outdated data, providing users with obsolete information

The Existential Threat to Public Media

  • Audience Migration: AI chatbots are increasingly substituting for direct website engagement, undermining both membership and underwriting models
  • The Substitution Effect: Research suggests approximately 80% of information queries can now be satisfied through conversational AI
  • Disintermediation Risk: Even when AI systems provide source attribution, users rarely engage with the original content

The Public Media Data Platform

Strategic Expansion

Extending this collaborative model to other major-market stations across the United States

Platform Economics

  • Network Effects: Each additional market increases the platform's comprehensiveness and commercial value
  • Local Specialization: Regional stations contribute irreplaceable geographic and cultural expertise
  • Shared Infrastructure: Distributed costs make sophisticated AI capabilities accessible to individual stations

Funding and Revenue Opportunities

  • AWS Imagine Grant: Infrastructure funding to build and demonstrate the platform
  • Innovation Demonstration: Showcase forward-thinking approach to attract additional foundation and donor support
  • Content Licensing: Potential agreements with AI companies for access to curated, authoritative local content
